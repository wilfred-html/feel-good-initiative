# Brand Identity Discovery — Summary
**Source:** Questionnaire_2.pdf (17 pages, completed by Ryan & Sandy)
**Date:** 25 March 2026
**Prepared by:** CloudSync Media (Pty) Ltd

## 1. Origin & Purpose
- **Founder:** Ryan Bluck — grew up in Hout Bay, daily beach walker, frustrated by rubbish
- **Mission:** Protect, restore, and beautify beaches & public spaces; create safe, accessible environments; bring community together; create jobs; inspire pride
- **Vision:** Scale from local cleanup to national model for environmental care + community upliftment
- **Values:** Community First, Environmental Stewardship, Consistency & Accountability, Local Pride, Growth & Opportunity
- **UVP:** Daily hands-on action (Beach Ranger every day), not occasional cleanups

## 2. Audience
- **Priority:** Corporate sponsors → Individual donors → Volunteers → Community → Government → Media
- **Ideal Sponsor:** Values community + environmental responsibility, wants visible impact, local or national
- **Current Sponsors:** Spar, Bokbus Tours, The Beach Bar, Deep Blue Security, Chas Everitt, Hout Bay Gallery, Handiman Hardware, The Beach Clinic, Alex Stewart
- **Community Perception:** Growing awareness, Sentinel coverage, curiosity + support

## 3. Brand Personality
- **Persona:** Energetic, approachable friend in mid-30s. Casual but purposeful. Confident without arrogance.
- **Spectrum:** Casual, Slightly Playful, Inclusive, Modern, Grassroots, Balanced/slightly urgent, Gentle but confident
- **SHOULD feel:** Empowering, Joyful, Bold, Connected, Vibrant
- **NEVER feel:** Dull, Closed-off, Helpless, Overbearing, Cynical
- **Avoid words:** "aid", "relief", "hand-me-downs", "we aim to", "small initiative", "budget-friendly", guilt-driven messaging

## 4. Visual Identity
- **Logo:** Existing but subtle — open to refinement for more impact
- **Colors:** Natural, clean, ocean/beach/outdoors tones (they defer to our expertise)
- **Imagery:** Real photos + professional/polished + drone shots
- **Style Balance:** Professional enough for sponsors, authentic enough to feel real
- **Physical touchpoints:** T-shirts, bins, billboards, expanding to banners, pitch decks, documents, signage

## 5. Messaging
- **Tagline ideas:** "Do Good. Feel Good." or "Real Impact. Real Change."
- **3 Key Messages:** Action protects environment, Community makes change possible, Positive action creates real impact
- **Money language:** Shift from "donate" to "invest in positive change" / "support our impact" / "partner"

## 6. Competitive Differentiation
- Holistic multi-front approach (not single-cause)
- Visible, local, immediate impact
- Sponsors as active partners, not just logos

## 7. Digital & Social
- Only on Facebook (144 followers) — need platform expansion guidance
- No content strategy yet — engaging us to build one
- Sensitive topics: politics, community tensions, exploitation

## 8. Brand Governance
- Authority: Sandy + Ryan
- Small team now, will expand to volunteers + external designers + sponsors
