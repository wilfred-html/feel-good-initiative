The Hout Bay Tidal Pool

A world-class eco-friendly tidal pool on Hout Bay Beach — designed to give safe ocean access to thousands of community members from Imizamo Yethu and Hangberg who currently lack it.

Natural Shoreline Integration

Blends into the natural coast, replacing an old revetment with a living tidal ecosystem.

Full Environmental Assessment

Marine biologists conducting a comprehensive impact study before any construction starts.

Ocean Safety & Marine Education

A hub for water safety training and marine ecology education for schools and communities.

Eco-Tourism Destination

Designed to become a landmark attraction for sustainable tourism in the Western Cape.

Our Mission

What We Stand For

The Feel Good Initiative bridges the gap between underserved communities and the natural spaces they deserve.

Ocean Access

Creating safe, inclusive coastal infrastructure for communities historically excluded from beach access.

Ocean Safety

Water safety education and drowning prevention — especially vital during summer months.

Conservation

Protecting marine ecosystems through science-led environmental assessments and community stewardship.

Education

Marine education programs that inspire the next generation to understand and protect our oceans.
